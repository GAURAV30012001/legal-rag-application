**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026

---

## 1. PURPOSE

The parties intend to explore a potential business collaboration regarding the development of an AI-powered legal analytics platform ("Purpose"). In furtherance of this Purpose, Discloser may share certain confidential and proprietary information with Recipient.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any data or information, oral or written, disclosed by Discloser to Recipient that is designated as confidential, or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure. This includes, without limitation:

- Trade secrets, source code, algorithms, and technical specifications
- Business plans, financial projections, pricing models
- Client lists, vendor details, and partnership agreements
- Research findings, prototypes, and product roadmaps
- Any information marked "Confidential", "Proprietary", or "Restricted"

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT

The Recipient agrees to:

1. Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent of the Discloser.
2. Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose stated herein.
3. Limit access to Confidential Information to employees or advisors who have a legitimate need to know and are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.
4. Promptly notify Discloser upon becoming aware of any actual or suspected breach of confidentiality.
5. Not reverse engineer, disassemble, or decompile any proprietary software or technology disclosed.

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does not include information that:

- Is or becomes publicly known through no wrongful act of Recipient
- Was already known to Recipient before disclosure, as evidenced by written records
- Is independently developed by Recipient without use of or reference to Discloser's information
- Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order — provided Recipient gives prior written notice to Discloser

---

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall remain in force for a period of **three (3) years** from the Effective Date unless earlier terminated by either party with thirty (30) days' written notice. Obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for an additional period of **two (2) years**.

---

## 6. RETURN OF INFORMATION

Upon termination or written request by Discloser, Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify in writing that no copies have been retained.

---

## 7. REMEDIES

Recipient acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to Discloser for which monetary damages would be inadequate. Discloser shall be entitled to seek equitable relief including injunctions and specific performance without the requirement of posting a bond.

---

## 8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising hereunder shall first be subject to mediation for thirty (30) days. If unresolved, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the seat of arbitration in New Delhi.

---

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and understandings, whether written or oral.
