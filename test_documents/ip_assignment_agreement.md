## RECITALS

A. The Inventor has created and developed certain patentable inventions, software, technical know-how, and related intellectual property in connection with an AI-based legal document analysis system ("Technology").

B. The Company desires to acquire ownership of the Technology and exploit it commercially, and the Inventor is willing to assign the same on the terms set forth in this Agreement.

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Intellectual Property Rights"** means all patents, utility models, copyrights, design rights, trade secrets, trademarks, database rights, domain names, and all other intellectual property rights, whether registered or unregistered, anywhere in the world.

**"Assigned IP"** means all Intellectual Property Rights in and to the Technology, including:
- Indian Patent Application No. 202521001234 titled "Method and System for AI-Driven Legal Document Classification"
- All related patent applications worldwide (continuations, divisionals, and PCT applications)
- Source code, object code, technical documentation, and training datasets
- Trade secrets, algorithms, and proprietary methodologies related to the Technology

**"Licensed IP"** means any background intellectual property owned by the Inventor prior to this Agreement that is necessary to exploit the Assigned IP.

---

## 2. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 2.1 Assignment

The Inventor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including the right to:
- File, prosecute, and maintain patent applications and registrations
- Sue for past, present, and future infringement
- License and sublicense the Assigned IP
- Modify, adapt, and create derivative works

### 2.2 Moral Rights

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, the Inventor waives all moral rights in the Assigned IP, including the right of integrity and the right of attribution.

### 2.3 Further Assurance

The Inventor shall execute any additional documents, assignments, powers of attorney, or declarations as the Company may reasonably require to perfect or record the assignment in any jurisdiction.

---

## 3. LICENSE-BACK TO INVENTOR

The Company grants the Inventor a non-exclusive, non-transferable, royalty-free, worldwide licence to use the Assigned IP solely for:
- Academic research and publication (non-commercial)
- Teaching and educational activities

The Inventor may not sublicense this licence. Any publication that makes use of the Assigned IP must acknowledge the Company's ownership.

---

## 4. CONSIDERATION

### 4.1 Upfront Payment

In consideration for the assignment, the Company shall pay the Inventor a one-time lump-sum of **INR 25,00,000 (Rupees Twenty-Five Lakhs)** within thirty (30) days of execution of this Agreement.

### 4.2 Revenue Share

In addition, the Company shall pay the Inventor a revenue share as follows:

| Annual Net Revenue from Technology | Royalty Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to INR 1 Crore | 5% |
| INR 1 Crore – INR 5 Crore | 3.5% |
| Above INR 5 Crore | 2% |

Revenue share payments shall be made quarterly within 45 days of quarter-end, accompanied by a statement of accounts.

### 4.3 Milestone Bonus

The Inventor shall receive an additional bonus of **INR 5,00,000** upon the grant of the first patent from the Assigned IP in any jurisdiction.

---

## 5. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

The Inventor represents and warrants that:

1. The Inventor is the sole and exclusive owner of the Assigned IP and has full authority to assign it
2. The Assigned IP does not infringe any third party's intellectual property rights
3. No claims, disputes, or litigation are pending or threatened with respect to the Assigned IP
4. The Assigned IP is not subject to any lien, encumbrance, or prior assignment
5. The Inventor has not licensed the Technology to any third party on an exclusive basis

---

## 6. INDEMNIFICATION

The Inventor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any claims, liabilities, damages, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from a breach of the warranties in Clause 5.

---

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party shall maintain the terms of this Agreement and all related technical and business information in strict confidence and shall not disclose the same to any third party without prior written consent, except as required by law.

---

## 8. TERM

This Agreement shall come into force on the date of execution and shall remain in force perpetually with respect to the assignment provisions. Revenue share obligations shall continue for seven (7) years from the date of first commercial sale of a product incorporating the Assigned IP.

---

## 9. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any dispute shall be resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The seat shall be Mumbai, the language shall be English, and the tribunal shall consist of a sole arbitrator appointed by mutual agreement.

---

## 10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement, together with its schedules, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and representations.
