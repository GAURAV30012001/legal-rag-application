# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Summary

This NDA requires the Recipient to keep Confidential Information strictly confidential and use it only for the Purpose defined in the agreement. The Recipient must protect the information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential data, and no less than a reasonable standard of care.

The NDA excludes information that becomes public through no fault of the Recipient, is already known, or is independently developed. The Recipient must notify the Discloser of any unauthorized access.

The confidentiality obligations last for three years from the Effective Date. Upon termination, the Recipient must return or destroy confidential materials upon request.
